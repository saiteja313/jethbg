About JET Harrisburg

Jeeyar Educational Trust Harrisburg is a community organization dedicated to spreading the divine teachings of HH Sri Chinna Jeeyar Swamiji.

We strive to preserve and promote our rich cultural and spiritual heritage through regular satsangams, festivals, and community service activities in the Harrisburg, PA area.
