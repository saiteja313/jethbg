Our Mission & Vision

Jeeyar Educational Trust Harrisburg (JET HBG) is a community organization committed to spiritual growth, cultural preservation, and selfless service in the greater Harrisburg, Pennsylvania area.

Under the divine guidance of His Holiness Sri Tridandi Srimannarayana Ramanuja Chinna Jeeyar Swamiji, we strive to spread the message of Bhagavad Ramanujacharya — that serving all beings is the highest form of service to God.

Our chapter organizes regular spiritual gatherings, cultural events, festival celebrations, educational programs for children, and community outreach activities.

About HH Sri Chinna Jeeyar Swamiji

Padma Bhushan Awardee — A beacon of spiritual wisdom and service

His Holiness Sri Tridandi Srimannarayana Ramanuja Chinna Jeeyar Swamiji is a revered spiritual leader, educator, and humanitarian recognized globally for his tireless efforts in promoting Vedic wisdom and serving humanity.

Swamiji was honored with the prestigious Padma Bhushan award by the Government of India in recognition of his extraordinary contributions to spirituality, education, and social welfare.

Under Swamiji's guidance, Jeeyar Educational Trust operates across multiple countries, running schools for blind and underprivileged children, conducting spiritual discourses, and organizing large-scale community events that bring together millions of devotees.

Swamiji's teachings emphasize that true devotion is expressed through service — "Serve All Beings As Service To God."

About Sri Bhagavad Ramanujacharya

Sri Bhagavad Ramanujacharya (1017–1137 CE) was a great philosopher, theologian, and social reformer who established the Vishishtadvaita (qualified non-dualism) school of Vedanta philosophy. His teachings emphasize devotion (bhakti) and surrender (prapatti) as paths to liberation, and he championed the equality of all beings before God.

Ramanujacharya's legacy lives on through institutions like the Jeeyar Educational Trust, which continues to spread his message of devotion, education, and service to all.
